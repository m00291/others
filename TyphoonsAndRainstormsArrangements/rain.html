颱風及暴雨教會聚會安排

1. 甚麼情況下取消聚會？
  • 如有以下任何一項，所有崇拜／聚會取消：
  • 八號或以上颱風信號已發出
  • 黑色暴雨警告信號已發出
  • 「極端情況」已發出
  • 天文台預告2小時內發出八號風球
  • 天文台預告八號風球生效時間距離聚會結束少於2小時
2. 崇拜／聚會 取消安排
信號「已發出」或

3. 紅色暴雨警告
  • 主日崇拜：照常進行
  • 青少年崇拜／其他聚會：取消
  • 如紅雨於聚會開始前2小時或更早解除，所有聚會照常
4. 其他警告信號
  • 所有聚會照常進行（除非教育局宣佈中學停課）
溫馨提示：
‣ 請時刻關注天氣變化，如有疑問請聯絡教會同工。
‣ 如遇緊急情況，請以安全為重！

Typhoons and Rainstorms Arrangements

1. When are worships and gatherings CANCELLED?
  • If any of the following applies, all worships and gatherings are cancelled:
  • Typhoon Signal No. 8 or above is issued
  • Black Rainstorm Warning Signal is issued
  • “Extreme Condition” announcement is issued
  • The Hong Kong Observatory announces that a Typhoon Signal No. 8 or above will be issued within 2 hours
  • The Hong Kong Observatory announces the time to issue a Typhoon Signal No. 8 or above is less than 2 hours after the end of the worship/gathering

3. Red Rainstorm Warning Signal is issued
  • Sunday worship will resume as scheduled.
  • Youth worship / other gatherings will be cancelled.
  • If, however, the Red Rainstorm Warning Signal is lowered at or before 2 hours of the worships / gatherings, all worships / gatherings will resume as scheduled.
4. Other Warning Signal is issued
  • All worships / gatherings will resume as scheduled. (unless Education Bureau announces that Secondary Schools are to close)
